Our Story

More Than Just a Café

Cup O' Joy was born from a simple belief — great food shouldn't cost a fortune, and every meal should be an experience worth remembering.

Nestled in the heart of Midnapore, we've become the go-to spot for students, families, and food lovers across the Medinipur region. Our industrial-chic space — complete with recycled bicycle wheel lights, rope-net ceilings, and warm Edison bulbs — is where creativity meets comfort.

From our signature Tandoori Chai served in authentic clay kulhads to our legendary Kurkure Momos, every item on our menu is crafted with love and served with joy.
